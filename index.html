Masjids in Berlin – Support

Welcome! This page provides support for the Masjids in Berlin mobile app.

Common Issues

  • Prayer times are inaccurate? Please ensure location access is enabled.
  • Can't find a masjid nearby? Try zooming out on the map or checking your GPS settings.

Contact

If you need help, feel free to email us at masjidsinberlin@gmail.com.

Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

Masjids in Berlin collects the following information:
• Location Information: We request access to your device's location to provide you with nearby masjid information and prayer times specific to your location.
All masjid information, prayer times, and other religious data provided in the app are collected from publicly available sources and are used for informational purposes only.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your location information solely to:
• Find and display nearby masjids
• Calculate accurate prayer times for your location
• Provide distance information to nearby masjids

3. Data Storage and Security

• Your location data is processed locally on your device and is not stored on our servers
• We do not share your location information with third parties
• You can revoke location permissions at any time through your device settings

4. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:
• Google Maps: For displaying maps and navigation features
• Expo Location: For accessing device location services

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:
• Access your location data
• Revoke location permissions
• Delete the app and all associated data

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
masjidsinberlin@gmail.com
